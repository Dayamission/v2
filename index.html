Welcome to Daya Mission

If there is love, unity, and cooperation among people, communities can experience hope and peace through the love of Christ. God placed a burden in our hearts to reach the unreached in the Eastern states of India. Since 2015, Daya Mission has been proclaiming the Gospel while serving communities through education, medical care, prayer, and acts of compassion.
