Welcome to Daya Mission

If there is love, unity and cooperation among people, communities can experience peace through the love of Christ. God placed a burden in our hearts to reach the unreached in the Eastern states of India. In 2015, Daya Mission was born with a vision of proclaiming the Gospel, strengthening believers, and serving people through compassion.
