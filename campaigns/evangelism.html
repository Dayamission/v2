Evangelism & Church Planting

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations..."

Sharing the Good News

The heart of Daya Mission is the proclamation of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We believe every person deserves the opportunity to hear about God's love and salvation through Jesus.

Our missionaries travel to villages and communities, sharing God's Word, encouraging believers, discipling new Christians, and helping establish local fellowships where people can worship and grow together.

What We Do

Proclaiming Christ and making disciples through biblical ministry.

Gospel Preaching

Sharing the message of salvation through Jesus Christ in villages, towns and communities where many have never heard the Gospel.

Discipleship

Teaching new believers to grow in God's Word, develop a strong prayer life and faithfully follow Christ.

Church Planting

Encouraging local fellowships and helping establish Christ-centered churches where believers can worship, grow and serve together.

Biblical Foundation

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you."

Matthew 28:19–20

Why Evangelism Matters

Jesus Christ commissioned His followers to proclaim the Good News to every nation and every people group.

Millions still live without hearing the Gospel. Through faithful evangelism, Daya Mission seeks to introduce people to Christ, encourage believers, and strengthen local churches.

Every outreach is an opportunity to share God's love, pray with families, distribute Bibles, and invite people into a relationship with Jesus.

Our Ministry Objectives

✔ Share the Gospel

✔ Strengthen Churches

Train faithful men and women for ministry and leadership.
