Prayer Meetings

Seeking God's presence through united prayer, worship and faith.

Standing Together in Prayer

Prayer is at the heart of every ministry at Daya Mission. Through regular prayer meetings, believers gather to worship God, seek His guidance, intercede for others, and encourage one another in faith.

We believe that God hears and answers prayer. Every gathering is an opportunity to experience His presence, receive strength, and pray for individuals, families, churches, missionaries, and nations.

As we pray together, we witness God's faithfulness, comfort, healing and transforming power.
