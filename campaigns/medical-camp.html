Medical Camps

Serving communities through compassionate healthcare and the love of Christ.

Healing with Compassion

Many families in rural and underserved communities have limited access to medical care. Through our Medical Camp ministry, Daya Mission partners with doctors, nurses and volunteers to provide free healthcare services.

Our medical camps offer consultations, health screenings, medicines, health education and prayer, demonstrating Christ's compassion for both physical and spiritual needs.

We believe that caring for people is an expression of God's love and opens doors to share the hope found in Jesus Christ.

Why Medical Camps Matter

Many rural communities have limited access to hospitals, qualified doctors and affordable medicines.

Our medical camps bridge this gap by bringing healthcare directly to people who might otherwise go without treatment.

Along with physical care, we share God's love through prayer, encouragement and compassionate service.

How Our Medical Camps Work

A simple, organized process to care for every patient.

1. Registration

Patients are welcomed and basic information is recorded.

2. Consultation

Doctors examine patients and recommend treatment.

3. Medicines

Patients receive prescribed medicines free of cost.

4. Prayer

Volunteers offer prayer, encouragement and spiritual support.
