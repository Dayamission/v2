Blanket Distribution

Sharing Christ's love by providing warmth, dignity and hope to families during the winter season.

Bringing Warmth with Compassion

Every winter, many families struggle to stay warm because they lack basic necessities. Through our Blanket Distribution ministry, Daya Mission reaches vulnerable people with blankets and shares the love of Jesus Christ.

Our volunteers visit villages, elderly people, widows, children, and families in need, offering not only blankets but also prayer, encouragement, and the message of hope found in Christ.

A simple blanket can become a powerful reminder that God cares for every individual.

Why This Ministry Matters

Demonstrating Christ's compassion through practical care.

Compassion

We care for vulnerable families by providing warmth during the cold season and reminding them that they are loved by God.

Community Care

Volunteers personally visit villages and communities, building relationships while serving with humility.

Sharing Christ's Love

Every blanket distributed is accompanied by prayer, encouragement and the message of hope found in Jesus Christ.

How We Serve

Our ministry team identifies villages and families who are most in need during the winter months.

With the support of generous partners, blankets are purchased, transported, and distributed directly to elderly people, widows, children, daily wage workers, and families facing financial hardship.

Each distribution event also includes prayer, encouragement, and whenever possible, sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
