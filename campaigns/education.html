Children's Education

Empowering children through education, faith and hope for a brighter future.

Educating Today's Children for Tomorrow's Leaders

Education is one of the greatest gifts we can offer a child. Through Daya Mission, we help children from economically challenged families gain access to education and opportunities for a brighter future.

Our ministry provides school supplies, educational support, Bible teaching, mentoring and encouragement so that every child can grow academically, spiritually and socially.

We believe every child is created in the image of God and deserves the opportunity to learn, dream and succeed.

What We Provide

Our education ministry supports children throughout the academic year by providing essential resources and encouragement.

  • 📚 School books and notebooks
  • 🎒 School bags and stationery
  • 👕 School uniforms (where needed)
  • 💡 Tuition and educational assistance
  • 📖 Bible lessons and character education
  • 🤝 Mentoring and personal encouragement
