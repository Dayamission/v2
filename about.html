Our Story

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations..."

Our Journey of Faith

Daya Mission began in 2015 with a burden placed by God to reach the unreached people in the Eastern states of India.

A small group of missionaries stepped out in faith, trusting the Lord to open doors for ministry. Although the work began with many challenges, opposition, and limited resources, God remained faithful.

Through the preaching of the Gospel, prayer, discipleship, and acts of compassion, many lives have been touched. Villages have heard the message of Christ, children have received educational support, families have experienced practical care, and believers have grown stronger in their faith.

Today, Daya Mission continues to serve communities by proclaiming the love of Jesus Christ through evangelism, education, medical outreach, prayer, and Bible teaching.

Our Vision

To glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers, planting churches, and transforming communities through Christ-centered compassion.

Our Mission

To share God's love through evangelism, discipleship, education, medical outreach, prayer, and community development, bringing hope to people in need.

Our Journey

God's faithfulness through the years.

2015

Daya Mission was founded with a vision to proclaim the Gospel and serve communities across Eastern India.

2017

Expanded village evangelism through Gospel meetings, discipleship and prayer fellowships.

2019

Introduced children's education support and Bible teaching ministries.

2021

Organized medical camps and compassion ministries for underserved communities.

Today

Continuing to share Christ's love through evangelism, discipleship, education, prayer, and community outreach.
