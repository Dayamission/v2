Our Ministries

Serving people through the Gospel of Jesus Christ, compassion and discipleship.

Transforming Lives Through Christ

Every ministry of Daya Mission is rooted in God's Word and driven by His love. We seek to proclaim the Gospel, care for people in need, strengthen believers, and bring hope to communities through practical acts of compassion.

Whether sharing the Gospel in villages, providing educational support, conducting medical camps, or encouraging believers through prayer, our desire is that Christ is glorified in everything we do.

Why We Serve

"For even the Son of Man came not to be served, but to serve, and to give His life as a ransom for many."

Mark 10:45

Every campaign of Daya Mission exists to glorify God and demonstrate the love of Jesus Christ through both the proclamation of the Gospel and practical acts of compassion.

Whether we are conducting medical camps, educating children, supporting struggling families, or sharing God's Word, our prayer is that every person encounters the transforming love of Christ.
